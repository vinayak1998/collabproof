# GST on barter collaborations (CGST Act 2017 + Rules) — summary with citations
[PLACEHOLDER SUMMARY — replace with official text before publishing results]

- s.7(1)(a) CGST: "supply" includes all forms of supply of goods or services made for a
  consideration in the course or furtherance of business — expressly including BARTER and
  exchange.
- s.2(31): "consideration" includes any payment made or to be done, in money OR OTHERWISE.
  A product transferred against a promotion deliverable is consideration for the creator's
  service; a purely gratuitous transfer with no reciprocal obligation is not consideration.
- Rule 27(a), CGST Rules: where the consideration is not wholly in money, the value of the
  supply is the OPEN MARKET VALUE of the supply.
- s.22(1): every supplier is liable to be registered in the State from which taxable supplies
  are made if aggregate turnover in a financial year exceeds Rs 20 lakh (Rs 10 lakh in
  special category states: Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland, Tripura). s.2(6): "aggregate
  turnover" includes the value of ALL taxable supplies — cash and barter consideration at
  open market value.
- Rate: influencer promotional/marketing services fall under SAC 9983 (other professional/
  advertising services) — 18%.
